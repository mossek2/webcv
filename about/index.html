So who am I?

My name is Kevin Mosse and I am a 21 year old computer science student living and studying in Dublin. Originally I am from Co. Kilkenny in the south of Ireland, however my educational adventure has brought me to Dublin City University. To say I love it here would be an understatement.

I'm a real people person. I love meeting and making new friends everywhere I go both at home and abroad. People who know me would consider me to be a pretty social and outgoing individual. I'm serious when it comes to the quality of my work and always organized, however outside of that not much else. I am definitely easy going and have glass half full outlook on life.

Passionate about computing, new business and technology from a young age. I'm driven by my love of learning and want of success. Everyday I want to learn something new and that coupled with my naturally entrapenureal spirit has motivated me to work hard at everything I do in life. I believe success starts with your mindset, and your eagerness to achive it.

When it comes to sport and fitness I can't get enough. I'm an early riser and 7.00am training sessions in the gym prepare me for the day ahead. I play football in Uni and have always been a diehard Man United supporter since I was a tot. Outside of soccer I've played rugby at schools level and won competeions playing tennis which I play obsessivly in the summer!

In my spare time I love to read, code and work on mini busineses. I activily take part in competitions such as Google Hashcode and DCU Hackaton. In addition to this I try to work on my own mini code projects and businesses outside of University which is pretty challenging but always fun.

Finally, I'm a very adventerous spirit. I love to travel and experience how people from different cultures do things. What I've seen and learned has inspired me to live life to the fullest and enjoy eveything I have!

Kevin Mosse 2017, Age 21
